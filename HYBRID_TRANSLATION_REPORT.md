# 🇮🇳 HYBRID TRANSLATION & TOGGLE UPDATE

## ✅ **Status: IMPLEMENTED**

I have upgraded the Translation Engine to **"Hybrid Mode"** and added the **Language Toggle**.

---

## 🗣️ 1. NEW: HYBRID TRANSLATION (Hinglish/Tanglish)
- **Problem**: Pure translations (e.g. *"Nivesh Kosh"* for Mutual Fund) are hard to understand.
- **Solution**: The AI now mixes **English Technical Terms** with **Regional Grammar**.
- **Example**: *"Mutual Funds mein SIP karna long-term wealth ke liye zaroori hai."*
- **Benefit**: Readable, Authoritative, and Native-feeling.

## 🌏 2. SUPPORTED LANGUAGES
- **Hindi** (हिंदी)
- **Telugu** (తెలుగు) - **NEW!**
- **Marathi** (मराठी)
- **Tamil** (தமிழ்)
- **Bengali** (বাংলা)
- **Gujarati** (ગુજરાતી)

## 🖱️ 3. LANGUAGE TOGGLE (HEADER NAV)
- **Location**: Top Navigation Bar (Next to "Compare Products").
- **Mobile**: Inside the Hamburger Menu.
- **Function**:
    - Detects if you are on an article (e.g., `/articles/best-sip`).
    - Switching to **Hindi** redirects to `/articles/best-sip-hi`.
    - If you are on the Home page, it stays on Home (future-ready for /hi home).
