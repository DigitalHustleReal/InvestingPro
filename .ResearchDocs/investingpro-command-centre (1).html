Deep Dive: The 4 Most Important Feature Specs

🔮CIBIL Score Simulator — Build Spec

Credit Karma's most-engaged feature globally. Nobody in India has built this. Users can run "what if" scenarios — pay off a debt, open a new card, close an old one — and see the estimated score impact instantly.

  • Input: current CIBIL score (300–900 slider) + outstanding card balances
  • "What if I pay card A to ₹0?" → shows estimated score increase (+X pts)
  • "What if I take a personal loan?" → shows estimated score drop
  • Leads directly into: "Here are the best cards at that score"
/tools/cibil-simulator — Input CIBIL + balances + recent enquiries → Output: "Paying ₹50K on Axis card → CIBIL improves ~18 pts in 60 days → You'd qualify for HDFC Infinia." Product recommendation at end.

📧Weekly Email Newsletter — Build Spec

MoneySavingExpert sends to 9M subscribers weekly. This single channel drives more direct traffic than Google for them. Zero Indian personal finance platform has built a serious email list. The biggest untapped moat available.

  • Curated: 5 financial tips relevant to this week (rate changes, new cards, tax deadlines)
  • Personalised segments: investors get MF news, home loan hunters get rate updates
  • Urgency layer: "RBI meets this week — FD rates may change. Lock in now?"
  • Drives direct traffic — immune to Google algorithm changes
InvestingPro Weekly — every Monday 9am. Segment 1: Rate Watch. Segment 2: Card of Week. Segment 3: Tax tip (seasonal). Build with Mailchimp. Target: 50K subs Year 1, 500K Year 3.

✅Approval Probability — Build Spec

ClearScore, MSE, and NerdWallet all show approval probability before you apply — without triggering a hard credit check. Reduces rejection anxiety and increases application rates significantly.

  • Input: CIBIL score range (soft input, not hard check), income, employment type
  • Output: green/amber/red indicator per card with approximate probability
  • "Apply with confidence" shows after probability displayed
  • Reduces wasted applications that further damage CIBIL score
Small badge on every card in comparison lists. "Your CIBIL 740 → Approval: Likely ✓ / Possible ~ / Unlikely ✗." Use publicly known eligibility criteria. No AA integration needed for v1 — rule-based logic from public bank data.

💰Affordability Score — Build Spec

MSE and ClearScore show an "Affordability Score" — not just credit eligibility, but whether you can actually afford the repayments. Nobody in India shows this. It positions InvestingPro as an actual financial advisor.

  • Input: monthly income, existing EMIs, rent/lifestyle expenses
  • Output: "Your safe loan EMI headroom is ₹14,000/month"
  • Translates to: "Based on that, home loans up to ₹18L fit your budget"
  • Prevents over-leveraging — a genuine trust signal to consumers
Embed in /loans/home-loan and /loans/personal-loan. "What's your take-home salary? Existing EMIs? → Safe borrowing capacity: ₹X. Here are the best rates for this range." Works with EMI calculator already in place.
