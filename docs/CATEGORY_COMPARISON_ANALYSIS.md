## Gap Analysis & Suitability for India

### ✅ HIGH PRIORITY - Add These Categories

#### 1. **Taxes** (CRITICAL for India)
**Why Essential:**
- Income Tax is mandatory for most salaried individuals
- GST affects all businesses and consumers
- Tax-saving investments (ELSS, PPF, NPS) are major financial decisions
- TDS, Advance Tax, ITR filing are complex topics
- Tax planning is a year-round concern

**Recommended Structure:**
- **Best**: Best Tax-Saving Investments, Best Tax Software, Best CA Services
- **Compare**: Tax-Saving Options Comparison, ITR Filing Platforms
- **Calculators**: Income Tax Calculator, GST Calculator, TDS Calculator, Tax-Saving Calculator
- **Guides**: ITR Filing Guide, Tax-Saving Investments Guide, GST Guide, Tax Planning Guide

#### 2. **Personal Finance** (HIGH VALUE)
**Why Essential:**
- Financial planning and budgeting are universal needs
- Wealth management and goal planning
- Emergency fund planning
- Debt management strategies
- Retirement planning (beyond just investments)
- Financial literacy content

**Recommended Structure:**
- **Best**: Best Financial Planning Tools, Best Budgeting Apps
- **Compare**: Financial Planning Approaches, Wealth Management Services
- **Calculators**: Budget Calculator, Emergency Fund Calculator, Debt Payoff Calculator
- **Guides**: Financial Planning Guide, Budgeting Guide, Debt Management Guide, Retirement Planning Guide

### ⚠️ MEDIUM PRIORITY - Consider These

#### 3. **Real Estate** (Could be separate or under Investing)
**Why Consider:**
- Property investment is a major financial decision in India
- Home buying process, documentation, legal aspects
- Rental yield calculations
- Property investment vs other assets
- Home loan integration (already under Loans)

**Decision:** Keep under Investing for now, but could expand later

### ❌ NOT SUITABLE - Skip These

#### 4. **Home** (as separate category)
**Why Skip:**
- Home Loans are already well-covered under Loans category
- Property buying guides can be under Investing or Personal Finance
- Not enough unique content to warrant separate category
- Indian market doesn't have the same mortgage refinancing culture as US
