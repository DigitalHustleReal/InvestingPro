# 1. TOPICAL AUTHORITY & SEO STRATEGY

## 1.1 Core SEO Philosophy: Depth Before Breadth

InvestingPro.in follows a **topical authority-first approach** aligned with Google's E-E-A-T guidelines and modern AI SEO best practices. We build **deep expertise in 2 categories first**, then expand systematically.

### The Topical Authority Playbook

```
WRONG APPROACH (Competitors):
├─ 10 articles in Credit Cards
├─ 10 articles in Mutual Funds
├─ 10 articles in Loans
├─ 10 articles in Insurance
└─ Result: No authority in any category = No rankings

RIGHT APPROACH (InvestingPro):
├─ 500+ articles in Credit Cards (FIRST)
├─ 500+ articles in Mutual Funds (FIRST)
├─ Result: Topical authority = #1 rankings
└─ THEN expand to other categories
```

## 1.2 Primary Categories (Months 1-12)

### Credit Cards — PRIMARY FOCUS
- **Target**: 500+ articles (Month 6) → 2000+ articles (Month 12)
- **Coverage**: Every spending pattern, lifestyle, income bracket
- **Examples**:
  - "Best credit card for ₹20K/month grocery spending"
  - "Best credit card for frequent travelers under ₹50K salary"
  - "HDFC Regalia Gold vs ICICI Sapphiro: Complete comparison"
- **Topical Clusters**: 50+ hub pages, 1000+ spoke articles
- **Goal**: #1 rankings for "best credit card" + 1000+ long-tail keywords

### Mutual Funds — PRIMARY FOCUS

## 1.5 Strategic Identity: Fintech vs Blog

**We are a Fintech Decision Platform. We are NOT a blog.**

| Feature | InvestingPro (Fintech) | Typical Finance Blog |
|:--------|:-----------------------|:---------------------|
| **Core Product** | Decision Engines & Calculators | Articles & Text |
| **User Goal** | "Tell me what to buy" (Action) | "Teach me how it works" (Education) |
| **Monetization** | High-Intent Affiliate (Credit Cards) | Low-Value Ads (AdSense) |
| **Valuation** | 10x-20x Revenue (SaaS multiples) | 2x-3x Profit (Media multiples) |
| **Tech Stack** | Next.js App, APIs, Database | WordPress Theme |

**Why this distinction matters:**
1.  **Affiliate Approval**: Networks approve "Platforms" instantly; they reject "Blogs" often.
2.  **User Trust**: Users trust "Tools" to give math-backed advice more than "Opinions".
3.  **Exit Strategy**: Use "Fintech" multiples for valuation, not "Content Site" multiples.

## 1.6 Mobile-First Platform Strategy

**Reality**: 90% of Indian personal finance users will access us via Mobile (Android).
**Strategy**: Desktop is a "port" of the Mobile App, not the other way around.

### The "Thumb-Zone" Promise
1.  **No Horizontal Scroll**: All tables (Comparison, Features) verify properly on 320px screens.
2.  **Bottom Navigation**: Critical actions (Compare, Apply, Menu) in the "Thumb Zone".
3.  **Speed**: <1s load time on 4G networks (Core Web Vitals > 90).
4.  **Touch Targets**: Buttons >44px height for fat fingers.
5.  **Data Visualization**: Charts stack vertically on mobile; no complex hover states.

**If it doesn't work on mobile, it doesn't exist.**

The Indian personal finance market is fragmented with no clear dominant player in the content + comparison space. Competitors fall into three categories:

1. **Transaction Platforms**: BankBazaar, PolicyBazaar, Paisabazaar (lead generation focus)
2. **Content Platforms**: Finology, Moneycontrol, ET Money (education/news focus)
3. **Trading Platforms**: Groww, Zerodha (transaction-first, content-second)

**Market Gap**: No platform combines **deep content authority + decision tools + instant application** in Credit Cards and Mutual Funds.

**Our Opportunity**: Become the NerdWallet of India by owning this gap.

## 4.4 Content Quality Framework

### Quality Scoring Algorithm (0-100)

```typescript
contentScore = (
  readabilityScore * 0.20 +    // Flesch-Kincaid, sentence length
  seoScore * 0.25 +             // Keywords, meta, schema, links
  structureScore * 0.15 +       // H2/H3, TOC, formatting
  affiliateScore * 0.20 +       // CTA count, placement, disclosure
  originalityScore * 0.10 +     // Plagiarism check, uniqueness
  engagementScore * 0.10        // Time on page, scroll, clicks
)
```

### Quality Tiers

| Tier | Score | Action | Example |
|:-----|:------|:-------|:--------|
| **Excellent** | 90-100 | Promote, expand, update regularly | Pillar pages, head terms |
| **Good** | 80-89 | Maintain, monitor | Most automated content |
| **Acceptable** | 70-79 | Optimize, improve | New content, testing |
| **Needs Work** | 40-69 | Flag for review, rewrite | AI drafts before QA |
| **Poor** | 0-39 | Archive, redirect, delete | Outdated, low-performing |

### Quality Gates (Must Pass to Publish)

1. ✅ **Minimum Score**: 70/100
2. ✅ **Word Count**: 2000+ words
3. ✅ **Plagiarism**: <10% similarity
4. ✅ **Affiliate CTAs**: 3+ per article
5. ✅ **SEO**: Meta tags, schema markup
6. ✅ **Links**: No broken links
7. ✅ **Images**: Featured image + alt text
8. ✅ **Readability**: Flesch-Kincaid >60

**Fail Rate**: <5% (most content passes on first try)

### 4.3 Legal Data Acquisition Strategy

**The Challenge**: We need product data (Fees, Rates, Features) to build comparison tools *before* we have official API access.
**The Risk**: Scraping competitors (BankBazaar/Paisabazaar) is legally risky (Copyright/TOS violations) and unreliable.

#### The "Safe Data" Protocol (Pre-Traction)

We follow a strictly **legal, white-hat data acquisition strategy** to build our initial database:

| Category | Primary Legal Source (Safe) | Secondary Method (Fair Use) | Do NOT Do (High Risk) |
|:---------|:----------------------------|:----------------------------|:----------------------|
| **Mutual Funds** | **AMFI / RBI (Official)**: Daily NAVs and scheme data are public domain. | **MF API (RapidAPI)**: Cheap, legal wrappers around official data. | Scraping Moneycontrol/Groww directly. |
| **Credit Cards** | **Bank Official Sites**: "Key Fact Statements" (PDFs). | **AI-Assisted Extraction**: Paste raw PDF text into ChatGPT/Claude → Get structured JSON. (Fast & 100% Legal). | Scraping BankBazaar's proprietary layout. |
| **Loans** | **Bank Websites**: Interest rate charts are public marketing material. | **Puppeteer (Headless)**: Visiting bank sites directly (not aggregators) to read rates. | Scraping aggregators like Paisabazaar. |
| **Product Images** | **Bank "Press Kits"**: Many banks offer official marketing assets. | **Fair Use Hosting**: Download official card image → Hosting on our CDN (Review/Comparison purpose is Fair Use). | Hotlinking directly to Bank/Competitor servers. |

#### Why This Works Legally:
1.  **Facts cannot be copyrighted**: Interest rates, fees, and feature lists are "facts". No one owns them.
2.  **Source Attribution**: We cite "Source: HDFC Bank Website" (builds trust).
3.  **No Aggregator Scraping**: We scrape the *source* (Banks), not the *middleman* (Competitors).
