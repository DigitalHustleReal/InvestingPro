## Full Product Verticals (8 Core Areas)

### 1. **Credit Cards** — Phase 1 (Now)
- **Comparison:** 500+ cards, 8 criteria, approval rings
- **Card vs Card:** 500 comparison pairs, programmatic generation
- **Reviews:** Deep per-card reviews with real experience
- **By Use Case:** OTT, fuel, dining, travel, rent, UPI rewards
- **Revenue:** ₹800–₹3,500 CPA per approved card application
- **Status:** Live, expanding

### 2. **CIBIL & Credit Health** — Phase 1 (Differentiator)
- **Free CIBIL Check:** Entry hook, no login required
- **CIBIL Simulator:** First in India — what-if scenarios
- **Improvement Plan:** 90-day personalized improvement roadmap
- **Dispute Tool:** RBI complaint letter generator
- **Revenue:** Indirect — converts to card/loan traffic
- **Status:** Simulator built, promoting

### 3. **Loans** — Phase 2 (High Value)
- **Home Loans:** Rate tables, EMI calc, DSA program integration
- **Personal Loans:** By CIBIL score, by salary range
- **Car & Education:** Comparison, eligibility checker
- **Loan Against:** MF, FD, property — underserved niche
- **Revenue:** 1–2% of disbursal amount — highest CPA potential
- **Status:** Planning partnerships

### 4. **Mutual Funds & SIP** — Phase 2
- **Fund Comparison:** Returns, expense ratio, risk rating
- **SIP Calculator:** Goal-based, inflation-adjusted
- **Index vs Active:** High-traffic debate content
- **SWP Planner:** Retirement withdrawal calculator
- **Revenue:** 0.5–1% AUM trail (compounds forever)
- **Status:** Tool building in progress

### 5. **Fixed Deposits & Savings** — Phase 2
- **FD Rate Table:** All banks, daily updated, auto-scraped
- **Savings Accounts:** Best interest rates, zero-balance options
- **FD Calculator:** Simple, compound, quarterly payout scenarios
- **Small Finance Banks:** 7–9% rates — underserved segment
- **Revenue:** ₹125–₹375 CPA per account opened
- **Status:** Scraper ready for launch

### 6. **Insurance** — Phase 3
- **Term Life:** India's most underinsured category (450K searches/mo)
- **Health Insurance:** Family floater vs individual plans
- **Motor Insurance:** High-volume, easy comparison
- **ULIP Analysis:** Why most ULIPs are bad deals (editorial stance)
- **Revenue:** ₹500–₹2,000 per policy lead
- **Status:** Content outline ready

### 7. **Tax & 80C Planning** — Phase 2
- **80C Calculator:** Which instrument saves most tax
- **Old vs New Regime:** Highest-search Indian tax query
- **ELSS vs PPF vs NPS:** Comparison content cluster
- **HRA / Home Loan:** Dual benefit calculators
- **Revenue:** Indirect — converts to MF/NPS traffic
- **Status:** Tax calculator live

### 8. **Programmatic SEO Tools** — Phase 1–2 Infrastructure
- **IFSC Lookup:** 80K pages, 1.29M visits/month potential
- **Bank Holidays:** 500 pages, bookmarked year-round
- **Gold Rate by City:** 100 pages, daily auto-update
- **PIN Code Hub:** 50K pages — local financial data layer
- **Finance Glossary:** 200 terms — Investopedia India equivalent
- **Status:** IFSC live, others in pipeline

### 9. **Calculators Hub** — Ongoing
- EMI (home, car, personal)
- SIP / SWP / CAGR
- Tax calculator + regime comparison
- CIBIL Simulator (flagship)
- Compound interest (FD, RD, PPF)
- Goal planner

## Six User Journey Archetypes

### 1. **The Spender** (22–35, salaried)
- **Entry:** "best cashback card" searches
- **Journey:** Compares cards → checks CIBIL → applies → later seeks loan
- **Content Needed:** Card comparisons, approval guides, CIBIL tips
- **Tools Needed:** CIBIL Simulator, card finder, approval predictor
- **Monetization:** Credit card CPA + later home loan

### 2. **The First-Time Investor** (28–40)
- **Entry:** "SIP kaise start karein" (how to start SIP)
- **Journey:** Reads MF guide → uses SIP calculator → starts SIP via ARN link
- **Content Needed:** MF basics, index funds, SIP guides, ELSS vs PPF
- **Tools Needed:** SIP calculator, goal planner, fund comparator
- **Monetization:** MF trail commission (long-term)

### 3. **The Home Loan Seeker** (30–45)
- **Entry:** "home loan rates 2026" / "CIBIL affect on home loan"
- **Journey:** Uses EMI calc → checks CIBIL impact → compares banks → applies via DSA
- **Content Needed:** Rate tables, CIBIL-to-rate guide, bank comparisons
- **Tools Needed:** EMI calculator, CIBIL simulator, prepayment calculator
- **Monetization:** Highest-value: 1–2% of disbursal

### 4. **The Tax-Stressed Professional** (25–50)
- **Entry:** Jan–March via "old vs new tax regime"
- **Journey:** Uses tax calculator → discovers 80C options → opens ELSS via MF link
- **Content Needed:** Regime comparison, 80C deep dives, HRA guides
- **Tools Needed:** Tax calculator, 80C planner, HRA calculator
- **Monetization:** Tax content → MF trail commission

### 5. **The FD Searcher** (40–65, conservative)
- **Entry:** "best FD rates today"
- **Journey:** Sees rate table → compares small finance banks → opens FD via affiliate
- **Content Needed:** Daily FD rate tables, safety ratings, SFB guides
- **Tools Needed:** FD calculator, laddering planner, rate alerts
- **Monetization:** ₹125–₹375 CPA per FD opened

### 6. **The Researcher** (all ages)
- **Entry:** IFSC code lookup / "what is CAGR"
- **Journey:** Reads glossary → bookmarks → returns for product decisions later
- **Content Needed:** Glossary, IFSC lookup, bank holidays, how-to guides
- **Tools Needed:** Search, IFSC tool, all calculators
- **Monetization:** Eventually converts to cards/loans

## What's Genuinely Missing (Critical Gaps)

### 1. **Insurance Cluster** — Not started
- **Opportunity:** India's biggest protection gap
- **Market Signal:** "Term insurance kya hota hai" = 450K searches/month
- **Problem:** Most Indians uninsured or mis-sold ULIPs
- **Edge:** Honest comparison page with clear "avoid ULIPs" editorial stance
- **Potential:** 50K+ visits Year 1
- **Start With:**
  - Term life vs ULIP guide
  - Health insurance floater vs individual

### 2. **NPS & Retirement Planning** — Not started
- **Opportunity:** NPS underrepresented on every Indian platform
- **Who Needs It:** Government employees, defence personnel, private sector workers
- **Content Cluster:** "NPS vs PPF vs ELSS" trio could own this space
- **Your Edge:** Defence/government employee context makes you credible
- **Start With:**
  - NPS calculator
  - Defence pension + NPS combo guide

### 3. **Community + WhatsApp** — Not built
- **Problem:** Entire strategy is SEO-first (Google-dependent)
- **Solution:** WhatsApp community of 500 engaged members = Google-proof
- **What to Send:** Rate alerts, weekly tips, new card launches
- **Model:** MSE email list adapted for India
- **Start With:** WhatsApp Business Community + weekly rate alert broadcast

### 4. **Email List** — Not launched (Critical)
- **Why:** Every platform studied (MSE, NerdWallet, Bankrate) treats email as #1 asset
- **Advantage:** Google-proof, algorithm-proof, ad-free distribution
- **Product:** "InvestingPro Weekly" newsletter
- **Timeline:** Should launch BEFORE first article
- **Start With:**
  - Resend account setup
  - Weekly email template
  - Signup form on every page

### 5. **Data / PR Reports** — Not planned
- **Model:** NerdWallet built 250+ high-DA backlinks via original data reports
- **Examples:** "Average credit card debt by Indian city" / "CIBIL score distribution by income"
- **Benefit:** Link building + brand building simultaneously
- **Press Angle:** ET and Mint cite sources that publish original research
- **Start With:** Quarterly India Finance Report + press outreach

### 6. **Gold & Sovereign Bonds** — Not covered
- **Traffic Opportunity:** "Aaj ka sone ka bhav" = millions of daily searches
- **Traffic Source:** BankBazaar and GoodReturns built massive traffic here
- **Potential:** 100 city-level gold rate pages = 200K+ visits/month (automated)
- **Affiliate Angle:** Sovereign Gold Bonds = legitimate investment with CPA
- **Start With:** Gold rate by city (100 pages), SGB vs physical gold guide
