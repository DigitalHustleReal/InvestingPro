**Last Updated:** January 23, 2026

## VISION

### Ultimate Vision
**"To become India's #1 Decision-Making Platform for Personal Finance"**

InvestingPro.in aims to be the largest personal finance platform in India within 2 years, operating across the full length and breadth of personal finance—helping users make informed financial decisions through comparison, recommendations, and instant application links.

### Strategic Positioning (Narrow-First Approach)
**"India's Decision-Making Platform for Credit Cards & Investments"**

**Strategic Rationale:** This is a **provisional, narrow-first positioning** to establish market dominance before expanding. The strategy is to:
1. **Go Narrow First:** Dominate Credit Cards + Mutual Funds (highest monetization potential)
2. **Establish Authority:** Build market leadership in primary categories
3. **Expand Strategically:** Add Insurance, Loans, Banking, and other categories AFTER primary categories are #1

**Why Narrow-First?**
- Focus resources on highest-revenue categories
- Build authority and brand recognition faster
- Establish competitive moat before competitors
- Prove monetization model works
- Then expand to dominate entire personal finance landscape
