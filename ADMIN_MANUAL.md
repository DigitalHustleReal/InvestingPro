
# InvestingPro Admin Console Manual (v2026)

## 1. Overview
The Admin Console is the "Cockpit" for the entire InvestingPro platform. It runs on a **Visual Engine First** architecture, meaning UI components are designed to look premium independently of data.

**URL**: `/admin`
**Theme**: Emerald/Teal (Fintech Premium)

---

## 2. Managing the Editorial Team (New!)
**Location**: `Content` > `Authors`

To create a credible publication, you need credible (or credible-looking) authors.
1.  Navigate to **/admin/authors**.
2.  Click **"Recruit Expert"**.
3.  **Name**: Enter a name (e.g., "Priya Sharma").
4.  **Role**: "Senior Market Analyst".
5.  **Photo**: Leave blank to auto-generate a generic avatar (DiceBear), or paste a real URL.
6.  **Socials**: Add Twitter/LinkedIn to enhance SEO schema.

**Why this matters**: Google E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) requires authors to have profiles. All articles generated by the Content Factory will be linked to these profiles.

---

## 3. Product Management (Rich Architecture)
**Location**: `Monetization` > `Product Catalog`

The platform supports "Rich Products" (Credit Cards with JSON features, ratings, pros/cons).
1.  Navigate to **/admin/products**.
2.  Click **"Add Product"**.
3.  **Tabs**:
    *   **Basic**: Name, Provider (Bank), Logo.
    *   **Details**: 
        *   **Rating**: 0-5 stars.
        *   **Pros/Cons**: Add bullet points (e.g., "Life-time Free", "High Forex Markup").
        *   **Key Features**: Add pairs like "Annual Fee": "₹500".
    *   **Links**: 
        *   **Affiliate Link**: The tracking URL (e.g., `vcommission...`).
4.  **Save**.

**Frontend Display**: These products automatically appear on `/credit-cards` and `/loans` in the "Universal Product Card".

---

## 4. The Content Factory (Automation)
**Location**: `Automation` > `Content Factory`

Generate hundreds of SEO-optimized articles in minutes.
1.  **Select Author**: Choose one of your "Dream Team" members.
2.  **Topics**: Enter keywords (e.g., "Best SIP for 2026").
3.  **Mode**: "Draft" (Review first) or "Publish" (Instant).
4.  **Ignite**: The system will:
    *   Research content via Gemini/OpenAI.
    *   Write the article with proper H1-H6 structure.
    *   Insert internal links to Products and Calculators.
    *   Assign the Author.

---

## 5. System Health & Calculators
*   **Calculators**: 13 Tools (SIP, GST, etc.) are self-contained. Manage their SEO content in the code (`SEOContent.tsx`), but they are largely maintenance-free.
*   **Affiliate Router**: Links like `/go/hdfc-regalia` will automatically redirect to the product's affiliate link.

- [ ] **Monthly**: Audit `Affiliate Clicks` in the Dashboard to see top performing products.
