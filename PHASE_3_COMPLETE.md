## 🎯 **HOW TO USE:**

### **1. Review Queue:**

2. Editors go to **Automation > Review Queue**.
3. Click "Review" to see content.
4. Click **"Approve"** (Auto-Publish) or **"Request Changes"** (Add notes).

### **2. A/B Experiments:**
1. Go to **Insights > Experiments**.
2. View identifying winning titles.
3. Create new tests to optimize SEO.
