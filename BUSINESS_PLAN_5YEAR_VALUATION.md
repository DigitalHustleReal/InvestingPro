## 💰 **REVENUE MODEL (Multi-Stream)**

### **1. Affiliate Commissions (Primary - 60-70% of revenue)**

**How It Works:**
- User compares products on InvestingPro
- Clicks "Apply Now" → Redirected to bank/lender/insurer
- If user completes application → We earn commission

**Commission Rates (Indian Market):**

| Product Category | Commission per Lead | Conversion Rate | Revenue per 1000 Visitors |
|------------------|---------------------|-----------------|---------------------------|
| **Credit Cards** | ₹500 - ₹2,000 | 2-5% | ₹1,000 - ₹10,000 |
| **Personal Loans** | ₹1,000 - ₹5,000 | 1-3% | ₹1,000 - ₹15,000 |
| **Home Loans** | ₹5,000 - ₹25,000 | 0.5-2% | ₹2,500 - ₹50,000 |
| **Insurance** | ₹500 - ₹5,000 | 2-4% | ₹1,000 - ₹20,000 |
| **Mutual Funds (SIP)** | ₹200 - ₹1,000 | 5-10% | ₹1,000 - ₹10,000 |
| **Demat Account** | ₹300 - ₹800 | 3-6% | ₹900 - ₹4,800 |
| **FD/RD** | ₹100 - ₹500 | 5-8% | ₹500 - ₹4,000 |
| **Business Loans** | ₹2,000 - ₹10,000 | 0.5-2% | ₹1,000 - ₹20,000 |

**Example Revenue Calculation:**
- **100,000 monthly visitors**
- **Average commission per visitor:** ₹15 ($0.18)
- **Monthly revenue:** ₹15,00,000 ($18,000)
- **Annual revenue:** ₹1.8 Crore ($216,000)
