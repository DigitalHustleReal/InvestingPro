## 🔗 **THE CONNECTED ECOSYSTEM**

### **1. 🏭 Generate (The Factory)**
- **New Feature**: Added a **"Generate with AI"** button to the main Articles page.
- **Action**: One click takes you to the Content Factory.
- **Output**: Generates full articles (Text + Images) instantly.

### **2. 📋 Manage (The List)**
- **Feature**: View all your generated articles in a WordPress-style list.
- **Status**: Newly generated articles appear as **Published** (or Draft if configured).
- **Actions**: Search, Filter, Delete, and Edit.

### **3. ✍️ Edit (The Studio)**
- **Feature**: Full WYSIWYG Editor (`/admin/articles/[id]/edit`).
- **Capability**: Tweak the AI-generated text, add new sections, or update SEO metadata before sharing.

### **4. 🌍 Publish (The Live Site)**
- **Feature**: articles are live at `/articles/[slug]`.
- **Sync**: Publishing updates the cache and makes it visible instantly.

## 🛠️ **HOW TO USE IT**

1.  Go to **[Articles Dashboard](http://localhost:3000/admin/articles)**.
2.  Click the **purple "Generate with AI"** button (top right).
3.  Enter a topic (e.g. *"Top 5 Credit Cards for Travel"*).
4.  Wait for generation.
5.  Go back to **Articles**, find your new post, and click **Edit** if you want to polish it, or **View** to see it live.
