## 📋 Page Creation Standards

### Core Principles:
- ✅ Trust-first, editorially disciplined
- ✅ Neutral, calm, explanatory tone
- ✅ No hype, urgency, or sales language
- ✅ Institutional quality
- ✅ Editor-reviewed content (assumed)

### Page Types:
1. **TYPE A**: Category Page (e.g., /investing/, /loans/)
2. **TYPE B**: Subcategory Page (e.g., /loans/home-loans/)
3. **TYPE C**: Guide/Explainer (e.g., "How SIP works")
4. **TYPE D**: Calculator Page (already done for SIP)

### Requirements:
- Internal links to parent category, related pages, glossary
- Clear statements: What it is, Who it's for, Who it's NOT for
- Key risks and limitations
- No affiliate CTAs
- No AI/automation references
- No emojis or casual language
- Last reviewed date
