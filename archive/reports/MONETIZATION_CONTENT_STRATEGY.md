## CONTENT TYPES (Decision-Focused)

### Credit Cards Content

**High-Value Topics (Publish First):**
1. "Best credit card for [spending amount] on [category]" (e.g., "Best credit card for ₹20K/month on groceries")
2. "Best credit card for [lifestyle]" (e.g., "Best credit card for frequent travelers")
3. "[Card A] vs [Card B] comparison" (e.g., "HDFC Regalia Gold vs ICICI Sapphiro")
4. "Best credit card with [feature]" (e.g., "Best credit card with lounge access")
5. "Best credit card for [income bracket]" (e.g., "Best credit card for ₹50K/month salary")

**Content Structure:**
- Problem statement (user's need)
- Comparison table (side-by-side)
- Recommendation (based on spending/lifestyle)
- "Apply Now" CTA (affiliate link)
- Pros/cons for each option

**Target:** 250+ articles in 90 days

---

### Mutual Funds Content

**High-Value Topics (Publish First):**
1. "Best SIP for [amount]/month for [goal]" (e.g., "Best SIP for ₹5000/month for retirement")
2. "Best mutual fund for [risk profile]" (e.g., "Best mutual fund for conservative investors")
3. "[Fund A] vs [Fund B] comparison" (e.g., "Parag Parikh Flexi Cap vs HDFC Balanced Advantage")
4. "Best mutual fund for [goal]" (e.g., "Best mutual fund for child's education")
5. "Best ELSS fund for tax saving" (tax as supporting, not primary)

**Content Structure:**
- Goal statement (user's objective)
- Risk assessment
- Fund comparison (returns, expense ratio, AUM)
- Recommendation (based on goal/risk)
- "Start SIP" CTA (affiliate link to Zerodha/Groww)
- Performance analysis

**Target:** 250+ articles in 90 days
