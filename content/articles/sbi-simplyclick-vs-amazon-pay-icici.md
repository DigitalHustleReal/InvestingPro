# SBI SimplyCLICK vs Amazon Pay ICICI — Best Shopping Card

These two cards dominate the online shopping credit card space in India. The Amazon Pay ICICI is the go-to for Amazon loyalists, while SBI SimplyCLICK rewards shopping across multiple platforms. Let us break down exactly which one deserves your wallet space.

## Key Takeaways

- Amazon Pay ICICI is lifetime free and delivers unbeatable 5% cashback on Amazon for Prime members
- SBI SimplyCLICK has a small annual fee but rewards shopping across 10+ partner platforms
- If Amazon is 60%+ of your online shopping, Amazon Pay ICICI wins clearly
- If you spread purchases across many platforms, SBI SimplyCLICK provides better overall returns
- Both cards are beginner-friendly with reasonable income requirements

## Detailed Analysis

### Reward Value: Amazon Shopping

For a monthly Amazon spend of ₹10,000:

**Amazon Pay ICICI (Prime member):**
- ₹10,000 x 5% = ₹500/month = **₹6,000/year**
- Minus annual fee: ₹0
- **Net annual value: ₹6,000**

**SBI SimplyCLICK:**
- ₹10,000 x 2.5% = ₹250/month = **₹3,000/year**
- Minus annual fee: ₹499 (assuming not waived)
- **Net annual value: ₹2,501**

**Winner for Amazon: Amazon Pay ICICI by ₹3,499**

### Reward Value: Multi-Platform Shopping

For monthly online spend of ₹15,000 spread across platforms (₹5,000 Amazon + ₹5,000 Flipkart + ₹5,000 others):

**Amazon Pay ICICI (Prime member):**
- Amazon: ₹5,000 x 5% = ₹250
- Flipkart: ₹5,000 x 2% = ₹100
- Others: ₹5,000 x 1% = ₹50
- Monthly total: ₹400 = **₹4,800/year**

**SBI SimplyCLICK:**
- Amazon: ₹5,000 x 2.5% = ₹125
- Cleartrip/Lenskart/others: ₹5,000 x 2.5% = ₹125
- Remaining online: ₹5,000 x 1.25% = ₹62.50
- Monthly total: ₹312.50 = ₹3,750/year
- Plus milestone benefit at ₹1L: ₹2,000
- Minus annual fee: ₹499
- **Net annual value: ₹5,251**

**Winner for multi-platform: SBI SimplyCLICK by ₹451** (narrow margin, and depends on which platforms you use)

### Cashback Mechanics

**Amazon Pay ICICI:** Cashback is credited as Amazon Pay balance within a few days. This can be used for Amazon purchases, bill payments, and at partner stores. It is essentially cash since Amazon Pay is widely accepted.

**SBI SimplyCLICK:** Earns reward points that need manual redemption through the SBI Card rewards catalog. Points can be redeemed for Amazon vouchers, merchandise, or statement credit. Redemption value varies — Amazon vouchers typically offer the best value.

**Winner: Amazon Pay ICICI** — Automatic cashback with zero redemption effort beats manual point redemption every time.

### Add-on Card Benefits

**SBI SimplyCLICK:** Free add-on cards for family members, earning rewards on the same account. All spending contributes to the milestone benefit threshold.

**Amazon Pay ICICI:** Add-on cards available with the same cashback benefits. Family spending contributes to total household Amazon savings.

**Winner: Tie** — Both offer useful add-on card options.

## Who Should Choose Which?

### Choose Amazon Pay ICICI If:

- You are an Amazon Prime member
- Amazon represents more than 50% of your online shopping
- You want a zero-cost card with no annual fee to worry about
- You prefer automatic cashback over manual redemption
- You want a solid 1% base rate on all non-Amazon spending

### Choose SBI SimplyCLICK If:

- You shop across multiple online platforms (not just Amazon)
- You are a regular user of Cleartrip, Lenskart, Netmeds, or other SBI partners
- You spend enough to hit the ₹1 lakh milestone for the ₹2,000 bonus
- You already bank with SBI (easier approval and relationship benefits)
- You value the higher online spend rate of 1.25% over Amazon Pay's 1% on non-partner sites

### The Smart Move: Get Both

These cards complement each other perfectly:
- **Amazon Pay ICICI** for all Amazon purchases (5% cashback)
- **SBI SimplyCLICK** for all other online shopping (1.25-2.5%)

Since the Amazon Pay ICICI is lifetime free, there is zero cost to holding both. If your SBI SimplyCLICK annual spend exceeds ₹1 lakh (across both online and offline), the fee gets reversed too.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I get both SBI SimplyCLICK and Amazon Pay ICICI?

Yes, there is no restriction on holding credit cards from different banks. Many savvy shoppers carry both and use each card where it delivers maximum rewards.

### Does Amazon Pay ICICI cashback work on Amazon Pantry and Fresh?

Yes, the 5% cashback (for Prime members) applies to all Amazon purchases including Amazon Pantry, Amazon Fresh, and Amazon Pay balance reloads.

### How do I redeem SBI SimplyCLICK reward points?

Log in to the SBI Card mobile app or website, navigate to "Rewards," and choose from Amazon vouchers, partner vouchers, merchandise, or statement credit. Amazon vouchers typically offer the best value per point.

### Is SBI SimplyCLICK worth it if I only shop on Amazon?

No. If Amazon is your primary shopping destination, the Amazon Pay ICICI delivers nearly double the cashback (5% vs 2.5%) at zero annual cost. SBI SimplyCLICK only makes sense if you shop significantly across other platforms.
