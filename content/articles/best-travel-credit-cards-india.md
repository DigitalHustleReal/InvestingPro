# Best Travel Credit Cards in India with Lounge Access

For frequent travellers, a good travel credit card is not a luxury — it is a money-saving tool. The right card can give you free airport lounge access, zero forex charges on international spending, complimentary travel insurance, and accelerated reward points on flights and hotels.

## Key Takeaways

- Travel credit cards save ₹1,500-3,000 per lounge visit — frequent flyers can save ₹20,000-50,000 annually
- Zero foreign currency markup cards save 3.5% on every international transaction
- Premium travel cards offer complimentary travel insurance worth ₹50 lakh to ₹3 crore
- Most travel cards require annual income of ₹6-15 lakh depending on the tier
- Reward points from travel cards are most valuable when redeemed for flights and hotel bookings

## Understanding Airport Lounge Access

### How Lounge Access Works

Travel credit cards provide lounge access through partnerships with lounge networks:
- **Priority Pass:** Global network of 1,500+ lounges in 140+ countries
- **Visa Infinite/Signature:** Access to Visa-affiliated lounges worldwide
- **Mastercard World:** Access to Mastercard lounge network
- **Domestic Networks:** DreamFolks, Lounge Key for Indian airport lounges

### What You Get in a Lounge

Airport lounges typically offer:
- Complimentary food and beverages (including alcohol at select lounges)
- Wi-Fi and charging stations
- Comfortable seating and quiet zones
- Shower facilities at premium lounges
- Newspapers, magazines, and TV

### Calculating Lounge Value

A single lounge visit costs ₹1,500-3,000 if purchased separately. If your card offers 12 free visits per year and you use all of them, the lounge benefit alone is worth ₹18,000-36,000 — far exceeding most annual fees.

## How to Choose a Travel Credit Card

1. **Count your annual trips:** 1-2 trips — entry-level card. 5+ trips — premium card with unlimited lounge access.
2. **Domestic vs international:** For domestic-only travel, lounge access matters most. For international travel, forex markup becomes critical.
3. **Calculate total travel spend:** If your annual travel spend exceeds ₹3-5 lakh, premium cards with higher reward rates deliver better net value.
4. **Check airline/hotel partnerships:** Some cards offer transfer partnerships with airlines (InterMiles, Air India) for better redemption value.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which credit card gives unlimited airport lounge access in India?

The HDFC Infinia and Axis Bank Magnus both offer unlimited domestic and international airport lounge access. The HDFC Regalia Gold and ICICI Sapphiro offer generous but capped lounge visits.

### Do travel credit cards cover international travel insurance?

Yes, most premium travel cards include complimentary travel insurance covering flight delays, lost baggage, medical emergencies, and air accident cover. Coverage typically ranges from ₹25 lakh to ₹3 crore depending on the card tier.

### Is it better to pay in INR or local currency abroad?

Always pay in local currency when using your credit card abroad. Choosing "pay in INR" at international terminals (Dynamic Currency Conversion) adds a hidden markup of 3-5% on top of your card's forex charges.

### Can I get lounge access for my family members?

Most cards provide lounge access only for the primary cardholder, though some extend one guest visit per complimentary visit. Add-on cardholders sometimes get separate lounge access. Check your specific card's terms for guest and add-on cardholder policies.
