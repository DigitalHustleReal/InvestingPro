# Best Credit Cards for Online Shopping in India

Online shopping in India has exploded, with Indians spending lakhs annually on e-commerce platforms. The right credit card can turn that spending into significant savings through cashback, reward points, and exclusive discounts.

## Key Takeaways

- Co-branded cards (Amazon Pay ICICI, Flipkart Axis) offer the highest returns on their respective platforms
- General online shopping cards like SBI SimplyCLICK reward spending across multiple platforms
- Most shopping cards offer accelerated rewards of 2-5% versus the standard 0.5-1% base rate
- Lifetime free cards can deliver excellent value without any annual fee commitment
- Stack credit card rewards with platform-specific sales (Big Billion Days, Great Indian Festival) for maximum savings

## Top Online Shopping Credit Cards

### 1. Amazon Pay ICICI Credit Card — Best for Amazon Shoppers

This remains India's most popular co-branded card with zero annual fee and industry-leading Amazon cashback.

- **Annual Fee:** Nil (lifetime free)
- **Amazon Cashback:** 5% for Prime members, 3% for non-Prime
- **Partner Sites:** 2% cashback on Swiggy, BookMyShow, Myntra, and others
- **All Other Spends:** 1% cashback
- **Welcome Benefit:** ₹250 Amazon Pay balance
- **Eligibility:** Income of ₹3 lakh+ per annum

**Why it tops our list:** Zero cost, automatic cashback credited as Amazon Pay balance, and no minimum redemption threshold. If Amazon is your primary shopping destination, this card is a no-brainer.

### 2. Flipkart Axis Bank Credit Card — Best for Flipkart & Myntra

- **Annual Fee:** ₹500 (waived on ₹2 lakh annual spend)
- **Flipkart/Myntra:** 5% cashback
- **Preferred Partners:** 4% cashback (Uber, PVR, Swiggy, others)
- **All Other Spends:** 1.5% cashback
- **Welcome Benefit:** ₹500 Flipkart voucher

**Best strategy:** Use during Flipkart Big Billion Days and Myntra End of Reason Sale for stacked savings of 5% card cashback plus platform discounts.

### 3. SBI SimplyCLICK Credit Card — Best Multi-Platform Card

- **Annual Fee:** ₹499 (reversed on ₹1 lakh annual spend)
- **Partner Platforms:** 2.5% back on Amazon, Cleartrip, Lenskart, Netmeds, and others
- **Other Online Spends:** 1.25% rewards
- **Offline Spends:** 0.25% rewards
- **Welcome Benefit:** ₹500 Amazon voucher
- **Milestone Benefit:** ₹2,000 worth of reward points on ₹1 lakh annual spend

**Why choose this:** If you shop across multiple platforms rather than being loyal to one, SBI SimplyCLICK offers the best spread of accelerated rewards. [Compare it with Amazon Pay ICICI](/articles/sbi-simplyclick-vs-amazon-pay-icici) for a detailed breakdown.

### 4. HDFC Millennia Credit Card — Best for Mixed Online/Offline

- **Annual Fee:** ₹1,000 (waived on ₹1 lakh annual spend)
- **Online Spends:** 2.5% cashback (on spends via PayZapp/SmartBuy)
- **Amazon/Flipkart/Sony LIV:** 2.5% cashback
- **Offline/Wallet Spends:** 1% cashback
- **Milestone Benefit:** ₹1,000 cashback on ₹1 lakh quarterly spend

### 5. Axis Bank ACE Credit Card — Best for Bill Payments

- **Annual Fee:** Nil (lifetime free)
- **Google Pay Transactions:** 5% cashback
- **Bill Payments & Utility:** 5% cashback via Google Pay
- **Dining/Travel:** 2% cashback
- **All Other Spends:** 1% cashback

**Pro tip:** Use this card linked to Google Pay for all online bill payments (electricity, mobile, broadband, DTH) for a flat 5% back.

## How to Maximize Shopping Card Rewards

### Stack Discounts Strategically

Layer your savings: platform sale discount + bank card offer + credit card cashback + coupon code. During major sales, this can yield 20-30% total savings.

### Use the Right Card for Each Platform

Many savvy shoppers carry 2-3 cards:
- Amazon Pay ICICI for Amazon
- Flipkart Axis for Flipkart/Myntra
- SBI SimplyCLICK or Axis ACE for everything else

### Time Big Purchases with Sales

Hold non-urgent purchases for Big Billion Days (October), Great Indian Festival (October), and Republic Day/Independence Day sales when card offers are stacked with platform discounts.

### Check Bank-Specific Offers

Before checkout, check your card's offer page. Banks frequently run 10% instant discount offers on specific platforms that stack with your regular rewards.

## Things to Watch Out For

- **Cashback caps:** Most cards cap cashback at ₹500-1,000 per month per category
- **Minimum transaction amounts:** Some offers require minimum spend of ₹1,000-5,000
- **EMI conversions:** Converting to EMI may void cashback on some cards
- **Reward point expiry:** Points expire after 2-3 years on most cards — redeem regularly

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which credit card gives the most cashback on Amazon?

The Amazon Pay ICICI card gives 5% cashback for Prime members and 3% for non-Prime members on all Amazon purchases. This is the highest consistent cashback available on Amazon India.

### Is the Flipkart Axis Bank card worth the ₹500 annual fee?

Yes, if you spend more than ₹10,000 annually on Flipkart and Myntra. The 5% cashback means you break even on the annual fee at just ₹10,000 spend. Plus, the fee is waived if you spend ₹2 lakh annually across all categories.

### Can I use multiple credit cards for online shopping?

Absolutely. Using platform-specific co-branded cards for their respective platforms and a general rewards card for other purchases is a proven strategy to maximize returns. This approach does not negatively impact your credit score.

### Do credit card offers stack with platform discounts?

Yes, in most cases credit card cashback is separate from platform discounts and coupon codes. During sale events, you can often combine platform discount + bank offer + card cashback for substantial total savings.
